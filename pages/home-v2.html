Support

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about tickets, venues, and the Laughix experience.

How do I book group tickets?

For groups of 10 or more, please contact our support team directly for special rates and seating arrangements.

Is there an age restriction for the shows?

While we recommend a 16+ audience due to themes, age restrictions vary by venue. Please check the specific show page for details.

What happens if a show is postponed?

Your tickets will automatically be valid for the new date. If you can't make it, a full refund will be available.

Can I record the performance?

To ensure the best experience for everyone, professional recording equipment is strictly prohibited. Phones are allowed but discouraged during the set.
